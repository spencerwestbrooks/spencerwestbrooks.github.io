Relapse: The Band

Relapse is a pop/soft rock teen band started in 2015 as The Profits of Harmony. In 2018, their named change into their current band name. They form part of Wired Music Productions[a school club] and Mmmc Records, Inc.[their label]. In January of 2018, they released their first cover song Memories on Spotify and iTunes. Later this year, in May, they released their original single Between Us, still as The Profits of Harmony. In early August, they started performing on stage as Relapse. They have worked for non-profit organizations like Make A Wish America, Relay for Life, Sheltering Tree Ranch, and others. They have been featured multiple times on the Pickwick VIP Magazine, and in the National Park Service website.
